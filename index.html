The world's worst synth

Play Hz

This is the world's worst synth, it uses the JavaScript Web Audio API to play 4 different oscillators at a frequency you choose.

It is based on Stuart Memo's blog post on making waves and his example.

This is the very beginning of what the Web Audio API can do. Better examples are the g200kg synthesizer or Stevie Graham's JPC2000.
